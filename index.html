About NSS NSUT
The National Service Scheme(NSS) Cell at Netaji Subhas University of Technology(NSUT) is a student-led initiative aimed at promoting social responsibility and community service among students. This cell is part of the larger NSS initiative which is a nation-wide program initiated by the Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government Of India. It conducts various activities like outreach programs, awareness campaigns, and volunteer efforts to foster societal development and encourage students to contribute to the well-being of the community.
